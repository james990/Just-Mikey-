Just Mikey



Mick Lennon A.K.A Just Mikey. Was born the 2/1/1990 Growing up in ballymun. He was influenced by the urban enviornment which surrounded him. He developed a love of rap music from an early age. And soon realised he could imitate other rappers songs with his own lyrics. He then start writing his own songs. Just Mikey met up with lok yip yau (Locky) a young music lover and an ambitous producer. The two lads clicked instantly and start making irish hip hop.

Mick Lennon has worked with a number of big name artists in the indusrty such as Redzer, Terawrizt, Nugget, Dizzy D and many more. With two albums under his belt (Micks'tape, Just Music) he his confident his newest album will be his best work to date. And is determined for the public to hear it.
